FAQ

Why yet another newsletter?

Why not? I want to share my learnings and experiments with others. Usually, I will tweet them or sometimes blog about them but I want to deliver it to you cutting the signal to noise ratio out there.



When can I expect an issue in my inbox?

I am expecting to send it once in two weeks on Fridays.


How is it going to be structured?

First few issues will be loosely structured just for me to experiment with the format.
I want to keep this newsletter concise and to the point. I will talk about software as well as other interesting things apart from software that will help you to be a better developer. Since I am not going to talk about any specific topic, I will include highlights in the beginning, so that the reader will get the idea of an issue right away.



How did I find this name YANL?

While I have a decent record of choosing cool names, I have to entirely give it to @dharm1t for suggesting this.
